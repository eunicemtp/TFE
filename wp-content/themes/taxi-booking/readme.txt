== Description ==

Taxi Booking is an excellent WordPress theme for showcasing cab services, online auto and cab booking, transportation and travel agencies, vehicles, taxi drivers, and more. Its minimalistic design showcases your company's ideals in the finest manner and has a polished finish. Its simple, responsive, and retina-ready design makes sure that your website loads smoothly and doesn't crop any of the content. Additionally, it is designed to be user-friendly so that novices may get started right away without having to write any code. Call to Action (CTA) Buttons: By increasing conversion rates, CTA buttons will enhance the value of your website. The theme has social network icons for additional promotion and SEO-friendly coding to increase traffic flow. A lighter design produced by the highly optimized coding guarantees a quicker page load time. With the theme settings panel's customisation options, you can effortlessly alter different features of this free theme, which is developed with the robust Bootstrap framework. This theme can be used to serve a variety of applications, such as a taxi booking app, hourly rental services, or WordPress sites. It is ideal for ordering and providing driver services for airport taxis, as well as for car rentals, car dealerships, mechanics, and limousine services. With the theme, visitors can easily manage their transportation needs and place online taxi orders for your taxi or transfer service business. It's the perfect theme for establishing an attractive landing page.
